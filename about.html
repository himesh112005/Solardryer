About SolarDry Solutions

Pioneering sustainable drying technology for a better future.

Our Mission

At SolarDry Solutions, we are committed to revolutionizing food preservation through innovative solar drying technology. Our mission is to empower farmers and businesses worldwide to reduce post-harvest loss, improve product quality, and increase profitability while protecting the environment.

Our Vision

We envision a world where sustainable drying technology is accessible to everyone, from small-scale farmers to large industrial operations. By harnessing the power of the sun, we aim to create a resilient food system that supports communities and protects our planet.

Why We Started

Post-harvest loss affects millions of farmers globally, leading to significant economic and environmental costs. We founded SolarDry Solutions to address this critical challenge by providing affordable, reliable, and sustainable drying solutions that make a real difference.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated engineers, environmentalists, and business professionals from SSVPS's B. S. Deore College of Engineering, working in partnership with La Fondation Dassault Systèmes to bring innovation and excellence to every project.
