A Simple, Powerful Process

Step 1

Collect

Solar energy is captured by a high-efficiency collector, heating the ambient air.

Step 2

Circulate

The hot, dry air is channeled into a sealed drying chamber, either passively or with fans.

Step 3

Dehydrate

The circulating air removes moisture from the produce, which is then vented out, preserving it naturally.
